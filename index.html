Albert Einstein

Theoretical physicist

Here's a time line of Albert Einstein life:

  • 1879 - Ulm, Kingdom of Württemberg, German Empire
  • 1895 - Moved to Switzerland, forsaking his German citizenship
  • 1897 - At the age of 17, he enrolled in the mathematics and physics teaching
    diploma program at the Swiss Federal polytechnic school in Zürich, graduating in 1900.
  • 1903 - Married Marić
  • 1905 - He was awarded a PhD by the University of Zurich
  • 1905 - Published four groundbreaking papers, outlining the theory of the theory of the photoelectric effect,
    explained Brownian motion, introduced special relativity, and demonstrated mass-energy equivalence.
  • 1914 - Moved to Berlin in order to join the Prussian Academy of Sciences and the Humboldt University of Berlin
  • 1916 - Published a paper on general relativity, introducing his theory of gravitation
  • 1917 - Applied the general theory of relativity to model the structure of the universe.
    He became director of the Kaiser Wilhelm Institute for Physics; he also became a German citizen again, this time Prussian.
  • 1922 - he was awarded the 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics
    "for his services to Theoretical Physics, and especially for his discovery
    of the law of the photoelectric effect"
  • 1940 - He became an American citizen
  • 1955 - He dies at the age of 76
